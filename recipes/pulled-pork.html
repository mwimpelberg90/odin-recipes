Pulled Pork Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp vegetable oil
  • 2 lb pork loin
  • 1 cup Stubbs BBQ Sauce
  • 2 tbsp worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 chopped onion

    • Cooking Steps

    1. Pour oil into the slow cooker, then place the roast on top of the oil.
    2. Add the remaining ingredients to the Crock-Pot.
    3. Shred the pork and return it to the slow cooker to combine it with the juices.
